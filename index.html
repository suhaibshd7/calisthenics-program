Training Rules
Intensity
RPE 6–7 most sets. Last set max RPE 8.
Holds
8–15 sec. Add time for tendon stiffness.
Reps
3–8 per set. RPE 8 ceiling always.
Volume
~10–14 hard sets. Set 1 = Set 4 quality.
Rest
2–3 min between sets.
Wrists
If sore, cut volume immediately.
WEEK 5
Deload
Cut sets ~30–40% · shorten holds · reduce intensity · never chase fatigue
Form Cues
Planche
  • Elbows locked hard
  • Push floor away
  • Protract scapula aggressively
  • Posterior pelvic tilt
  • Ribs down
  • Lean from shoulders
  • Wrists active, fingers spread
Front Lever
  • Straight arms
  • Scapula depressed
  • Posterior pelvic tilt
  • Ribs tucked
  • Glutes tight
  • Body rigid — no arch
